F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to enroll?

    Enrollment is done through our online portal by registering or walk in at any of locally registered agent. Enrollees will receive a unique pin from Recyclables.

  • How to earn money or buy products from the store?

    Registered recyclers would receive SMS on value credited to their earning points after dropping off recyclables.

  • What can the earning points be used for?

    Recyclers can use their earning points to get cash, shopping vouchers, phone top-up or thrift items from our online store.

  • How can we drop off recyclables?

    This can be done either through pickup or drop off. Pickups can be scheduled online or through SMS and phone calls.

  • Can pickup be done for small quantity of items?

    Pick ups are only done for bulk recyclables.

  • How do we know the points for each recyclable item?

    Below is our points table;

    Products Points
    Plastic bottles 15
    Water Sachets/Nylon 10
    Cartons 50
    Jars/glass bottles 200
    Books, Newspapers 20
    Tins/cans 100
